Eaton IQ35M
Technical Data: TD02601018E

The IQ 35M meter provides the ability to monitor volts and amps per phase, power, and energy. It meets ANSI C12.20 standards for accuracy of 0.5 percent and has optional Modbus output and data logging.

General Specifications:

  • Programmable to monitor and display current, voltage, frequency, power factor, power
    (real, reactive, apparent), energy (watt-hours, var hours, VA hours), and system demand.

  • Standard KYZ pulse output

  • Optional Modbus / data logging.

  • 0.5% Class meter.

  • Designed for basic feeder applications where reading of voltage, current, frequency, power, and energy are needed

  • Alarm notification on display

  • Single line LCD display

Eaton IQ230
Technical Data: TD02601015E

The IQ 210 meter is programmable to monitor and display phase currents, voltage, power (real, reactive, apparent), energy (watt-hours, var hours, VA hours), frequency, power factor, min and max values, system demand. 240V AC maximum system voltage. 1.0% Class energy meter with backlit LCD display. The IQ 220 offers the same features as the IQ 210 except rated for 600V AC maximum system voltage and also has INCOM communication capability and KYZ pulse output. The IQ 230 offers the same features as the IQ 220 plus 2 digital inputs and 2 digital outputs.

General Specifications:

  • Smaller size is ideal for panelboard and switchboard applications where panel space is at a premium and ANSI C12.16 accuracy is required

  • Energy management information provided by direct reading of up to 79 metered values

  • Can be used as a metering transducer for submetering applications

  • IQ210-Programmable to monitor and display phase currents, voltage, power (real, reactive, apparent), energy (watt-hours, var hours, VA hours), frequency, power factor, min and max values, system demand. 240V AC maximum system voltage. 1.0% Class energy meter with backlit LCD display.

  • IQ220-Same features as the IQ 210 except rated for 600V AC maximum system voltage. Also has INCOM communication capability and KYZ pulse output.

  • IQ230-Same features as the IQ 220 plus 2 digital inputs, 2 digital outputs, and 1 Analog Input.
